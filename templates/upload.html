Applicant Resume Analyzer (ARA)

This application analyzes resumes using NLP to identify suitable job roles and profile strength.

What we do

  • Resume parsing & validation
  • Job role identification
  • Experience analysis
  • Resume scoring

How it works

  1. Upload your resume
  2. Enter experience
  3. ARA processes your profile
  4. Email notification sent

Things to keep in mind while uploading

  • Upload your resume in PDF format only
  • Ensure the resume file name is Your Full Name
  • Use a clear and simple resume format
  • Mention skills and experience clearly
  • Avoid images, tables, or heavy graphics
  • Ensure contact details (email & phone) are present

Your resume data is secure, confidential, and used only for analysis purposes.
